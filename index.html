About

I am an applied AI engineer and research software engineer with more than ten years of experience building practical systems across cybersecurity, blockchain, data analytics and enterprise software.

I am currently pursuing a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Sydney. My research examines the resilience and security of decentralised infrastructure, with an emphasis on turning research findings into dependable tools and working systems.

My work spans agentic AI for security, graph machine learning, privacy, blockchain applications, IoT analytics and large-scale data modelling. I enjoy working between research and engineering, particularly on problems where technical rigour must meet real operational constraints.
